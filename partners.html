Red River Software

Red River Software is an award-winning technology development business that has been always been based in Horsham and now operates at an international level. In addition to many education-based initiatives and apprenticeships they offer, Red River MD, Simon Pringle recently won Gatwick Diamond Business Person of the Year 2016, is Chair of the Horsham Digital Hub Steering Committee, and is also collaborating to bring TeenTech in the Gatwick Diamond region.

Hack Horsham

Founded in January 2015 by two local parents whose experience in the technology field set the directions for HackHorsham. Created as a non-profit organisation it has helped to build the momentum leading to the digital hub concept. We have organised and delivered over 40+ STEM workshops & events which inspired children and adults to create, produce and discover the world of STEM. Working with many different organisations; HackHorsham is the glue which joins exciting visionary ideas with inventors and opportunities.
